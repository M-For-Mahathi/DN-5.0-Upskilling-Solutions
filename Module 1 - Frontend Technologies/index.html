Bulletin

News and updates from Greenfield City Council.

The Greenfield City Council is pleased to announce a series of community events throughout May and June. Residents are encouraged to participate in the upcoming Baking Workshop at the Community Centre on May 18th. Limited seats are available, so early registration is advised. The annual Cultural Night returns to Town Hall on May 24th, featuring performances from local dance and music groups. Entry is ₹50 per person, with children under 12 admitted free. Sports Day at City Park on June 1st is open to all age groups. Teams can register through this portal. The Health Camp on June 8th will offer free general check-ups, blood pressure monitoring, and dietary advice from qualified professionals. The Music Festival on June 15th at the City Amphitheatre promises an evening of live performances. Advance tickets are ₹100. All event proceeds go towards the Community Development Fund, which supports local infrastructure and social programmes.
